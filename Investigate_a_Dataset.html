#first : does the 'gender' factor affect on patients attendance?
def attendance(df,col_name,attended,absent):
    plt.figure(figsize=[15, 10])
    df[col_name][show].hist(alpha=0.7, color='blue', label='show')
    df[col_name][noshow].hist(alpha=0.7, color='yellow', label='noshow')
    plt.legend();
    plt.title('attendance according to gender')
    plt.xlabel('Gender')
    plt.ylabel('patients attendance');
attendance(df,'Gender',show,noshow)

Females who attend were more than males who attend , also the females who does not attend were more than the males who does not attend¶

So,Gender has no effect on patients attendance¶
